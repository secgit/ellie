How to Play

1. Move the princess around using arrow keys, gamepad, touch/swipe, or tilt controls

2. Go to the Coffee Station (left) to make items

3. Count how many items the customer needs

4. Go to the Delivery Zone (right) to serve the customer

5. Complete orders to earn points!
